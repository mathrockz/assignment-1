Who is Peter Griffin?

Peter Griffin is the lead character in the popular, long-running animated television program Family Guy. He is the patriarchal figure of the Griffin family, consisting of father Peter, wife and mother Lois, children Chris, Meg, and Stewie, and dog Brian. Background

Peter Griffin lives in a humble McMansion style home in the fictional city of Quahog, Rhode Island. Not much is known about his early childhood except the fact that he was born in Mexico. Due to conflicting information, it is assumed that he is between the ages of 43 and 58.

Occupation

For the first three seasons of Family Guy, Peter is employed at the Happy-Go-Lucky Toy Factory. For the most recent seasons, Peter is employed at the Pawtucket Brewery. He was initially working on the bottling line, but after being unable to keep his hands off the infinite supply of beer, he was demoted to working a desk job at the shipping center.
